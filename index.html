Butter Chicken

Butter chicken (Murgh Makhani) is an iconic Indian dish featuring marinated, tandoor-cooked chicken simmered in a velvety, spiced, and buttery tomato-cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 Cardamoms
  • 1 inch Cinnamon
  • 2-3 Black Peppercorns
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • ½ Cup Tomato Puree
  • ½ tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • ½ tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • ¼ Cup Kasoori Methi
  • ½ Cup Yogurt
  • 400gms Chicken Tikka Pieces

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add all the whole spices and sauté for few seconds.
  2. Now ginger garlic paste and sauté till fragrant.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook till it leaves oil.
  4. Now add red chili powder, salt, sugar, garam masala powder, kasoori methi and mix well.
  5. Now add in yogurt, give a nice stir. Add in chicken tikka pieces and cook for more 2-4mins. Turn Off the flame and serve hot.
